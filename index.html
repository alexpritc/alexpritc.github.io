Pacific Parkour League (PPL)


PPL is a first person parkour platformer game I made as my entry for Ludum Dare 40. However, the final product was also published on itch.io for the wider public to enjoy.

Ludum Dare is an event where you create a game from scratch in a weekend based on a theme. The theme was "the more you have, the worse it is". In PPL, complete extreme parkour levels with a crazy amount of momentum. The more momentum, the harder it is!
