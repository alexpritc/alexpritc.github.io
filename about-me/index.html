There'll never be enough time to read all the books I want, or listen to all of the great podcasts I'm subscribed to. I just dedicate so much of my time to writing, designing and coding.


Software Development

I'm currently studying Computing and Games Development at Plymouth University. I have developed games using industry-standard tools (such as Unity) and iterative, agile, development methods. I have also worked with pre-release development hardware, such as the HTC Vive Pre, to test and improve my ideas.

Having previously studied Psychology, I have the upper-hand in the development of human-computer interfaces as I can incorporate a variety of psychological approaches. As Rick Cook said, “Programming today is a race between software engineers striving to build bigger and better idiot-proof programs, and the Universe trying to produce bigger and better idiots. So far, the Universe is winning.”

Although scientifically minded, I have artistic hobbies; these include creative writing and design. Applying these skills, I'm bringing fresh ideas to both Computer Science and Gaming. However, there is more to this vast and continuously changing industry, than purely entertainment. Developing software for other purposes e.g. VR simulations for pilots and trainee medical professionals, is also an exciting prospect.
